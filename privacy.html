Required permissions

Tsacdop require following permissions on you devices.

  • INTERNET -used for search podcasts, get podcast update, play and download episodes.
  • ACCESS NETWORK SATE -used for play & download callular data use setting.
  • WRITE & READ EXTERNAL STORAGE -used for download episodes and offline play.
  • FOREGROUND SERVICE -used for auto update podcasts in background.
